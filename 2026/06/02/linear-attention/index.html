2.1

Gated DeltaNet 的更新公式：

$$
\mathbf{S}t = \mathbf{S}{t-1} \alpha_t \left( \mathbf{I} - \beta_t \mathbf{k}_t \mathbf{k}_t^\top \right) + \beta_t \mathbf{v}_t \mathbf{k}_t^\top
$$

仿照 DeltaNet 和 GLA 中的推导，定义

$$
\mathbf{P}{[t]}^{,r} = \prod{i=tC+1}^{tC+r} \left( \mathbf{I} - \beta_i \mathbf{k}i \mathbf{k}i^\top \right) \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times d}, \qquad \gamma{[t]}^{,r} = \prod{i=tC+1}^{tC+r} \alpha_i
$$

$$
\mathbf{H}{[t]}^{,r} = \sum{i=tC+1}^{tC+r} \beta_i \left( \mathbf{v}i \mathbf{k}i^\top \right) \frac{\gamma{[t]}{,r}}{\gamma_{[t]}{,i}} \left( \prod{j=i+1}^{tC+r} \left( \mathbf{I} - \beta_j \mathbf{k}_j \mathbf{k}_j^\top \right) \right) \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times d}
$$

于是，分块后的状态可以写成：

$$
\mathbf{S}{[t]}^{,r} = \mathbf{S}{[t-1]}^{,C} , \gamma_{[t]}^{,r} \mathbf{P}{[t]}^{,r} + \mathbf{H}{[t]}^{,r}, \qquad \text{where } \mathbf{S}_{[-1]}^{,C} = 0
$$
