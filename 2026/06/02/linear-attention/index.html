1.1

Delta Net的更新公式
$$
S_t = S_{t-1}(\mathbf{I} - \beta_t k_t k_t^\top) + \beta_t v_t k_t^\top = \sum_{i=1}^t \beta_i(v_i k_i^\top) \left( \prod_{j=i+1}^t (\mathbf{I} - \beta_j k_j k_j^\top) \right).
$$

$$
P_j^i = \prod_{t=i}^j (\mathbf{I} - \beta_t k_t k_t^\top) \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times d}, \quad
\mathbf{H}i^j = \sum{t=i}^j \beta_t (v_t k_t^\top) P_{t+1}^j \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times d}
$$

开始推导
$$
S_{t}=S_{t-1}(I-\beta_{t}k_{t}k_{t}^T)+\beta_{t}v_{t}k_{t}^T
=S_{t-1}+\beta_{t}(v_{t}-S_{t-1}k_{t})k_{t}^T
$$
展开递推式
$$
S_{1}=\beta_{1}v_{1}k_{1}^T
$$

$$
S_{2}=S_{1}(I-\beta_{2}k_{2}k_{2}^T)+\beta_{2}v_{2}k_{2}^T
=\beta_{1}v_{1}k_{1}^T(I-\beta_{2}k_{2}k_{2}^T)+\beta_{2}v_{2}k_{2}^T
$$

$$
S_{t}=\sum_{i=1}^t[\beta_{i}(v_{i}k_{i}^T)\prod_{j=i+1}^t(I-\beta_{j}k_{j}k_{j}^T)]
$$
