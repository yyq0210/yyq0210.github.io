1 Delta Net

论文链接：[2406.06484] Parallelizing Linear Transformers with the Delta Rule over Sequence Length

1.1

Delta Net的更新公式

$$S_t = S_{t-1}(\mathbf{I} - \beta_t k_t k_t^\top) + \beta_t v_t k_t^\top = \sum_{i=1}^t \beta_i(v_i k_i^\top) \left( \prod_{j=i+1}^t (\mathbf{I} - \beta_j k_j k_j^\top) \right). $$$$P_j^i = \prod_{t=i}^j (\mathbf{I} - \beta_t k_t k_t^\top) \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times d}, \quad \mathbf{H}_i^j = \sum_{t=i}^j \beta_t (v_t k_t^\top) P_{t+1}^j \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times d} $$

开始推导

$$S_{t}=S_{t-1}(I-\beta_{t}k_{t}k_{t}^T)+\beta_{t}v_{t}k_{t}^T =S_{t-1}+\beta_{t}(v_{t}-S_{t-1}k_{t})k_{t}^T $$

展开递推式

$$S_{1}=\beta_{1}v_{1}k_{1}^T $$$$S_{2}=S_{1}(I-\beta_{2}k_{2}k_{2}^T)+\beta_{2}v_{2}k_{2}^T =\beta_{1}v_{1}k_{1}^T(I-\beta_{2}k_{2}k_{2}^T)+\beta_{2}v_{2}k_{2}^T $$$$S_{t}=\sum_{i=1}^t[\beta_{i}(v_{i}k_{i}^T)\prod_{j=i+1}^t(I-\beta_{j}k_{j}k_{j}^T)] $$

1.2

定义如下变量

$$P_{i}^j=\prod_{t=i}^j(I-\beta_{t}k_{t}k_{t}^T)\in\mathbb{R}^{d\times d} $$$$H_{i}^j=\sum_{t=i}^j\beta_{t}(v_{t}k_{t}^T)P_{t+1}^j\in\mathbb{R}^{d\times d} $$$$\mathbf{S}_{[t]}^r = \mathbf{S}_{[t]}^0 \mathbf{P}_{[t]}^r + \mathbf{H}_{[t]}^r $$

开始推导

$$S_{tC+r}=H_{1}^{tC+r}=\sum_{t=1}^{tC+r}\beta_{t}(v_{t}k_{t}^T)P_{t+1}^{tC+r} =\sum_{t=1}^{tC}\beta_{t}(v_{t}k_{t}^T)P_{t+1}^{tC+r}+\sum_{t=tC+1}^{tC+r}\beta_{t}(v_{t}k_{t}^T)P_{t+1}^{tC+r} $$$$P_{a}^{c}=P_{a}^{b} P_{b}^c $$$$S_{tC+r}=[\sum_{t=1}^{tC}\beta_{t}(v_{t}k_{t}^T)P_{t+1}^{tC}]P_{tC+1}^{tC+r}+\sum_{t=tC+1}^{tC+r}\beta_{t}(v_{t}k_{t}^T)P_{t+1}^{tC+r}=S_{tC}P_{tC+1}^{tC+r}+H_{tC+1}^{tC+r} $$$$S_{[t]}^{r}=S_{[t]}^{0}P_{[t]}^{r}+H_{[t]}^{r} $$

1.3

引入变化

$$\begin{aligned} \mathbf{P}_{[t]}^r &= \mathbf{I} - \sum_{i=1}^r \mathbf{w}_{[t]}^i \mathbf{k}_{[t]}^{i^T}, \\ \mathbf{H}_{[t]}^r &= \sum_{i=1}^r \mathbf{u}_{[t]}^i \mathbf{k}_{[t]}^{i^T} & \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times d} \end{aligned} $$$$\begin{aligned} \mathbf{w}_{[t]}^r &= \beta_{[t]}^r \left( \mathbf{k}_{[t]}^r - \sum_{i=1}^{r-1} \left( \mathbf{w}_{[t]}^i (\mathbf{k}_{[t]}^{i^T} \mathbf{k}_{[t]}^r) \right) \right) & \in \mathbb{R}^d \end{aligned} $$$$\begin{aligned} \mathbf{u}_{[t]}^r &= \beta_{[t]}^r \left( \mathbf{v}_{[t]}^r - \sum_{i=1}^{r-1} \left( \mathbf{u}_{[t]}^i (\mathbf{k}_{[t]}^{i^T} \mathbf{k}_{[t]}^r) \right) \right) & \in \mathbb{R}^d \end{aligned} $$

开始推导

  • 定义
$$\prod_{i=1}^{t} (\mathbf{I} - \beta_i \mathbf{k}_i \mathbf{k}_i^\top) = \mathbf{I} - \sum_{i=1}^{t} \mathbf{w}_i \mathbf{k}_i^\top $$
  • 数学归纳法得
$$\begin{aligned} \mathbf{P}_n &= \mathbf{P}_{n-1} (\mathbf{I} - \beta_n \mathbf{k}_n \mathbf{k}_n^\top) \\ &= \left(\mathbf{I} - \sum_{t=1}^{n-1} \mathbf{w}_t \mathbf{k}_t^\top\right) (\mathbf{I} - \beta_n \mathbf{k}_n \mathbf{k}_n^\top) \\ &= \mathbf{I} - \sum_{t=1}^{n-1} \mathbf{w}_t \mathbf{k}_t^\top - \beta_n \mathbf{k}_n \mathbf{k}_n^\top + \left(\sum_{t=1}^{n-1} \mathbf{w}_t \mathbf{k}_t^\top\right) \beta_n \mathbf{k}_n \mathbf{k}_n^\top \\ &= \mathbf{I} - \sum_{t=1}^{n-1} \mathbf{w}_t \mathbf{k}_t^\top - \underbrace{\left(\beta_n \mathbf{k}_n - \beta_n \sum_{t=1}^{n-1} \left(\mathbf{w}_t (\mathbf{k}_t^\top \mathbf{k}_n)\right)\right)}_{\mathbf{w}_n} \mathbf{k}_n^\top \\ &= \mathbf{I} - \sum_{t=1}^{n} \mathbf{w}_t \mathbf{k}_t^\top \end{aligned} $$

同理，我们可以通过归纳法证明 ：

$$S_n = \sum_{i=1}^n u_i k_i^T $$$$\begin{aligned} S_n &= S_{n-1} (I - \beta_n k_n k_n^T) + \beta_n v_n k_n^T \\ &= \left( \sum_{t=1}^{n-1} u_t k_t^T \right) (I - \beta_n k_n k_n^T) + \beta_n v_n k_n^T \\ &= \sum_{t=1}^{n-1} u_t k_t^T - \left( \sum_{t=1}^{n-1} u_t k_t^T \right) \beta_n k_n k_n^T + \beta_n v_n k_n^T \\ &= \sum_{t=1}^{n-1} u_t k_t^T + \left( \beta_n v_n - \beta_n \sum_{t=1}^{n-1} u_t (k_t^T k_n) \right) k_n^T \\ &= \sum_{t=1}^n u_t k_t^T \end{aligned} $$

1.4

代入得到最终公式如下：

$$\mathbf{S}_{[t]}^r = \mathbf{S}_{[t]}^0 + \sum_{i=1}^r \left( \mathbf{u}_{[t]}^i - \mathbf{S}_{[t]}^0 \mathbf{w}_{[t]}^i \right) \mathbf{k}_{[t]}^{i\top}, $$$$\mathbf{o}_{[t]}^r = \mathbf{S}_{[t]}^r \mathbf{q}_{[t]}^r = \mathbf{S}_{[t]}^0 \mathbf{q}_{[t]}^r + \sum_{i=1}^r \left( \mathbf{u}_{[t]}^i - \mathbf{S}_{[t]}^0 \mathbf{w}_{[t]}^i \right) \left( \mathbf{k}_{[t]}^{i\top} \mathbf{q}_{[t]}^r \right). $$

令

$$S_{[t]} = S_{[t]}^0 $$

则

$$S_{[t+1]} = S_{[t]} + \left( U_{[t+1]} - W_{[t+1]} S_{[t]}^T \right)^T K_{[t+1]} $$$$O_{[t+1]} = Q_{[t+1]} S_{[t]}^T + \left( Q_{[t+1]} K_{[t+1]}^T \odot M \right) \left( U_{[t+1]} - W_{[t+1]} S_{[t]}^T \right) $$

1.5

其中 u、w 的求解采用 UT 变换，其中 $M_{-1} = M - I$ 表示对角线元素为 0 的严格下三角掩码矩阵。

$$u_{[t]}^{r} = \beta_{[t]}^{r} \left( v_{[t]}^{r} - \sum_{i=1}^{r-1} u_{[t]}^{i} \, k_{[t]}^{i\top} \, k_{[t]}^{r} \right) $$$$\Rightarrow \mathbf{U}_{[t]} = \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \mathbf{V}_{[t]} - \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \mathbf{K}_{[t]} \mathbf{K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \mathbf{U}_{[t]} $$$$\Rightarrow \left( I + \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \mathbf{K}_{[t]} \mathbf{K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \right) \mathbf{U}_{[t]} = \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \mathbf{V}_{[t]} $$$$\Rightarrow \mathbf{U}_{[t]} = \left( I + \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \mathbf{K}_{[t]} \mathbf{K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \right)^{-1} \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \mathbf{V}_{[t]} $$

同理：

$$\mathbf{W}_{[t]} = \left( I + \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \mathbf{K}_{[t]} \mathbf{K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \right)^{-1} \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \mathbf{K}_{[t]} $$

因此，要求 $\mathbf{U}_{[t]}$、$\mathbf{W}_{[t]}$，核心在于计算：

$$\mathbf{T}_{[t]} = \left( I + \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \mathbf{K}_{[t]} \mathbf{K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \right)^{-1} \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) $$

2 Gated Delta Net

论文链接：arxiv.org/pdf/2412.06464

2.1

Gated DeltaNet 的更新公式：

$$\mathbf{S}_t = \mathbf{S}_{t-1} \alpha_t \left( \mathbf{I} - \beta_t \mathbf{k}_t \mathbf{k}_t^\top \right) + \beta_t \mathbf{v}_t \mathbf{k}_t^\top $$

仿照 DeltaNet 和 GLA 中的推导，定义

$$\mathbf{P}_{[t]}^{\,r} = \prod_{i=tC+1}^{tC+r} \left( \mathbf{I} - \beta_i \mathbf{k}_i \mathbf{k}_i^\top \right) \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times d}, \qquad \gamma_{[t]}^{\,r} = \prod_{i=tC+1}^{tC+r} \alpha_i $$$$\mathbf{H}_{[t]}^{\,r} = \sum_{i=tC+1}^{tC+r} \beta_i \left( \mathbf{v}_i \mathbf{k}_i^\top \right) \frac{\gamma_{[t]}^{\,r}}{\gamma_{[t]}^{\,i}} \left( \prod_{j=i+1}^{tC+r} \left( \mathbf{I} - \beta_j \mathbf{k}_j \mathbf{k}_j^\top \right) \right) \in \mathbb{R}^{d \times d} $$

于是，分块后的状态可以写成：

$$\mathbf{S}_{[t]}^{\,r} = \mathbf{S}_{[t-1]}^{\,C} \, \gamma_{[t]}^{\,r} \mathbf{P}_{[t]}^{\,r} + \mathbf{H}_{[t]}^{\,r}, \qquad \text{where } \mathbf{S}_{[-1]}^{\,C} = 0 $$

2.2

这跟1.3是一样的，假设

$$\mathbf H_t = \eta_t \sum_{i=1}^{t} \mathbf u_i^{0} \mathbf k_i^\top $$

那么由数学归纳法可得：

$$\mathbf H_t = \alpha_t \mathbf H_{t-1} + \beta_t \left( \mathbf v_t - \alpha_t \mathbf H_{t-1}\mathbf k_t \right) \mathbf k_t^\top = \alpha_t \eta_{t-1} \sum_{i=1}^{t-1} \mathbf u_i^0 \mathbf k_i^\top + \beta_t \left( \mathbf v_t - \alpha_t \eta_{t-1} \sum_{i=1}^{t-1} \mathbf u_i^0 \mathbf k_i^\top \mathbf k_t \right) \mathbf k_t^\top = \eta_t \sum_{i=1}^{t} \mathbf u_i^0 \mathbf k_i^\top $$

其中：

$$\eta_t = \alpha_t \eta_{t-1} = \gamma_t, \qquad \mathbf u_t^0 = \frac{\beta_t}{\eta_t}\mathbf v_t - \beta_t \sum_{i=1}^{t-1} \mathbf u_i^0 \mathbf k_i^\top \mathbf k_t $$

等价地，也可以写成：

$$\mathbf H_t = \sum_{i=1}^{t} \frac{\gamma_t}{\gamma_i} \mathbf u_i \mathbf k_i^\top, \qquad \mathbf u_t = \beta_t \left( \mathbf v_t - \sum_{i=1}^{t-1} \frac{\gamma_t}{\gamma_i} \mathbf u_i \mathbf k_i^\top \mathbf k_t \right) $$

P同理

$$\mathbf P_{[t]}^{\,r} = \mathbf I - \sum_{i=1}^{r} \mathbf w_{[t]}^{\,i} \mathbf k_{[t]}^{\,i\top}, \qquad \mathbf w_{[t]}^{\,r} = \beta_{[t]}^{\,r}\mathbf k_{[t]}^{\,r} - \beta_{[t]}^{\,r} \sum_{i=1}^{r-1} \mathbf w_{[t]}^{\,i} \mathbf k_{[t]}^{\,i\top} \mathbf k_{[t]}^{\,r} $$

2.3

代回到 chunkwise 递推式中，就有

$$\begin{aligned} \mathbf S_{[t]}^{\,r} &= \mathbf S_{[t-1]}^{\,C} \gamma_{[t]}^{\,r} \left( \mathbf I - \sum_{i=1}^{r} \mathbf w_{[t]}^{\,i} \mathbf k_{[t]}^{\,i\top} \right) + \sum_{i=1}^{r} \frac{\gamma_{[t]}^{\,r}}{\gamma_{[t]}^{\,i}} \mathbf u_{[t]}^{\,i} \mathbf k_{[t]}^{\,i\top} \\ &= \gamma_{[t]}^{\,r} \mathbf S_{[t-1]}^{\,C} + \gamma_{[t]}^{\,r} \sum_{i=1}^{r} \left( \mathbf u_{[t]}^{\,i} - \mathbf S_{[t-1]}^{\,C} \gamma_{[t]}^{\,i} \mathbf w_{[t]}^{\,i} \right) \frac{\mathbf k_{[t]}^{\,i\top}}{\gamma_{[t]}^{\,i}} \end{aligned} $$$$\begin{aligned} \mathbf o_{[t]}^{\,r} &= \mathbf S_{[t]}^{\,r} \mathbf q_{[t]}^{\,r} \\ &= \mathbf S_{[t-1]}^{\,C} \mathbf q_{[t]}^{\,r} \gamma_{[t]}^{\,r} + \sum_{i=1}^{r} \left( \mathbf u_{[t]}^{\,i} - \mathbf S_{[t-1]}^{\,C} \gamma_{[t]}^{\,i} \mathbf w_{[t]}^{\,i} \right) \frac{\mathbf k_{[t]}^{\,i\top}}{\gamma_{[t]}^{\,i}} \mathbf q_{[t]}^{\,r} \gamma_{[t]}^{\,r} \end{aligned} $$

2.4

现在再定义：

$$\overleftarrow{\mathbf X}_{[t]} = \operatorname{Diag}(\gamma_{[t]}) \mathbf X_{[t]}, \qquad \overrightarrow{\mathbf X}_{[t]} = \operatorname{Diag}(\gamma_{[t]})^{-1} \mathbf X_{[t]}, \qquad \text{for } \mathbf X \in \{\mathbf Q,\mathbf K,\mathbf W\} $$

于是，整体计算可以进一步写成矩阵形式：

$$\mathbf S_{[t]}^{C} = \gamma_{[t]}^{C} \mathbf S_{[t-1]}^{C} + \gamma_{[t]}^{C} \left( \mathbf U_{[t]}^{\top} - \mathbf S_{[t-1]}^{C} \overleftarrow{\mathbf W}_{[t]}^{\top} \right) \overrightarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]} \mathbf O_{[t]} = \overleftarrow{\mathbf Q}_{[t]} \mathbf S_{[t-1]}^{C\top} + \left( \overleftarrow{\mathbf Q}_{[t]} \overrightarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot \mathbf M \right) \left( \mathbf U_{[t]} - \overleftarrow{\mathbf W}_{[t]} \mathbf S_{[t-1]}^{C\top} \right) $$

2.5

接下来处理 U、W，采用 UT 变换求解 $\mathbf{u}_{[t]}$ 和 $\mathbf{w}_{[t]}$。

其中 $M_{-1}=M-I$ 表示主对角线为零的严格下三角掩码。

从 $\mathbf{u}_{[t]}$ 的递推式出发，可以得到：

$$\mathbf u_{[t]}^{\,r} = \beta_{[t]}^{\,r} \left( \mathbf v_{[t]}^{\,r} - \sum_{i=1}^{r-1} \frac{\gamma_{[t]}^{\,r}} {\gamma_{[t]}^{\,i}} \mathbf u_{[t]}^{\,i} \mathbf k_{[t]}^{\,i\top} \mathbf k_{[t]}^{\,r} \right) $$$$\begin{aligned} \Rightarrow \mathbf U_{[t]} &= \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \mathbf V_{[t]} - \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \overleftarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]} \overrightarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \mathbf U_{[t]} \\ \Rightarrow \left( \mathbf I + \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \overleftarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]} \overrightarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \right) \mathbf U_{[t]} &= \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \mathbf V_{[t]} \\ \Rightarrow \mathbf U_{[t]} &= \left( \mathbf I + \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \overleftarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]} \overrightarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \right)^{-1} \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \mathbf V_{[t]} \end{aligned} $$

同理，也有：

$$\mathbf W_{[t]} = \left( \mathbf I + \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \overleftarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]} \overrightarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \right)^{-1} \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \mathbf K_{[t]} $$

因此，要计算 U 和 W，关键就在于下面两个量：

$$\mathbf T_{[t]} = \left( \mathbf I + \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \mathbf K_{[t]} \mathbf K_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \right)^{-1} \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) $$$$\widetilde{\mathbf T}_{[t]} = \left( \mathbf I + \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \overleftarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]} \overrightarrow{\mathbf K}_{[t]}^{\top} \odot M_{-1} \right) \right)^{-1} \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) $$

需要注意的是，这个表达式与原论文中的写法是等价的：

$$\widetilde{\mathbf T}_{[t]} = \left( \mathbf I + \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \Bigl( ( \mathbf K_{[t]} \mathbf K_{[t]}^{\top} \odot \Gamma_{[t]} ) \odot M_{-1} \Bigr) \right)^{-1} \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) $$

其中

$$(\Gamma_{[t]})_{ij} = \frac{\gamma_i}{\gamma_j}. $$

3 Kimi Delta Attention

论文链接：arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26692

3.1

原始公式：

$$\mathbf S_t = \mathbf S_{t-1} \operatorname{Diag}(\alpha_t) \left( \mathbf I - \beta_t \mathbf k_t \mathbf k_t^\top \right) + \beta_t \mathbf v_t \mathbf k_t^\top \in \mathbb R^{d_v\times d_k} \mathbf o_t = \mathbf S_t \mathbf q_t $$

接下来，遵循 DeltaNet 的推导，定义块级符号和以下辅助量：

$$\mathbf P_{[t]}^{\,r} = \prod_{i=tC+1}^{tC+r} \operatorname{Diag}(\alpha_i) \left( \mathbf I - \beta_i \mathbf k_i \mathbf k_i^\top \right) \in \mathbb R^{d_k\times d_k} $$$$\mathbf H_{[t]}^{\,r} = \sum_{i=tC+1}^{tC+r} \beta_i \left( \mathbf v_i \mathbf k_i^\top \right) \prod_{j=i+1}^{tC+r} \operatorname{Diag}(\alpha_j) \left( \mathbf I - \beta_j \mathbf k_j \mathbf k_j^\top \right) \in \mathbb R^{d_v\times d_k} $$

然后，块级状态可以写为：

$$\mathbf S_{[t]}^{\,r} = \mathbf S_{[t-1]}^{\,C} \mathbf P_{[t]}^{\,r} + \mathbf H_{[t]}^{\,r}, \qquad \text{where } \mathbf S_{[-1]}^{\,C} = 0 $$

3.2

定义

$$P_{[t]}^{\,r} = \operatorname{Diag} \!\left( \gamma_{[t]}^{\,r} \right) - \sum_{i=1}^{r} \operatorname{Diag} \!\left( \gamma_{[t]}^{\,i\to r} \right) k_{[t]}^{\,i} w_{[t]}^{\,i\top} H_{[t]}^{\,r} = \sum_{i=1}^{r} \operatorname{Diag} \!\left( \gamma_{[t]}^{\,i\to r} \right) k_{[t]}^{\,i} u_{[t]}^{\,i\top} $$

其中

$$w_{[t]}^{\,r} = \beta_{[t]}^{\,r} \left( \operatorname{Diag}(\gamma_{[t]}^{\,r}) k_{[t]}^{\,r} - \sum_{i=1}^{r-1} w_{[t]}^{\,i} \left( k_{[t]}^{\,i\top} \operatorname{Diag}(\gamma_{[t]}^{\,i\to r}) k_{[t]}^{\,r} \right) \right) u_{[t]}^{\,r} = \beta_{[t]}^{\,r} \left( v_{[t]}^{\,r} - \sum_{i=1}^{r-1} u_{[t]}^{\,i} \left( k_{[t]}^{\,i\top} \operatorname{Diag}(\gamma_{[t]}^{\,i\to r}) k_{[t]}^{\,r} \right) \right) $$

3.3

UT 变换

$$M_{[t]} = \left( I + \operatorname{StrictTril} \left( \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) \left( \Gamma_{[t]}^{1\to C} \odot K_{[t]} \right) \left( \frac{K_{[t]}} {\Gamma_{[t]}^{1\to C}} \right)^{\!\top} \right) \right)^{-1} \operatorname{Diag}(\beta_{[t]}) $$$$W_{[t]} = M_{[t]} \left( \Gamma_{[t]}^{1\to C} \odot K_{[t]} \right), \qquad U_{[t]} = M_{[t]}V_{[t]} $$

3.4

最终的 chunk-wise 表达式

$$S_{[t+1]} = \operatorname{Diag} \!\left( \gamma_{[t]}^{C} \right) S_{[t]} + \left( \Gamma_{[t]}^{1\to C} \odot K_{[t]} \right)^{\!\top} \left( U_{[t]} - W_{[t]}S_{[t]} \right) \in \mathbb{R}^{d_k\times d_v} $$$$O_{[t]} = \left( \Gamma_{[t]}^{1\to C} \odot Q_{[t]} \right) S_{[t]} + \operatorname{Tril} \left( \left( \Gamma_{[t]}^{1\to C} \odot Q_{[t]} \right) \left( \frac{K_{[t]}} {\Gamma_{[t]}^{1\to C}} \right)^{\!\top} \right) \left( U_{[t]} - W_{[t]}S_{[t]} \right) \in \mathbb{R}^{C\times d_v} $$
